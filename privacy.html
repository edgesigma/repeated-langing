Privacy policy

Last updated July 15, 2026

repeated is a voice-first social app. This page explains what we collect and why, in plain language.

What we collect

  • Your Google account's email address and identifier: used only to sign you in. We never post on your behalf or read anything else from your Google account.
  • Your handle, your speeches (as text), and your voice mark (as geometry): this is your public content in the app.
  • Usage analytics: anonymized events (like "a speech was posted" or "a recording was retried") via Google Analytics for Firebase, so we can find what's broken and fix it.

What we don't collect

  • Audio. Your voice is processed on your device to produce text and drawings. Recordings never leave your phone and are never stored.
  • Contacts, location, photos, or anything else the app doesn't need.

Where your data lives

Content and account data are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), subject to Google's data processing terms. Waitlist emails submitted on this site are stored the same way and used only to contact you about repeated.

Deleting your data

You can request deletion of your account and everything attached to it; see Delete your account. Waitlist removal: email us and we'll delete your entry.
