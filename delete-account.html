Delete your account

Deleting your repeated account removes your profile, your handle, your voice mark, all your speeches, and your repetitions. This can't be undone; your handle becomes available for someone else to claim.

How to request deletion

  1. Email yell@mauricewingfield.com with the subject "Delete my repeated account".
  2. Send it from the Google account you sign in with, and include your @handle so we can verify it's you.
  3. We'll confirm and complete the deletion within 30 days, usually much sooner.

What's deleted, what's kept

Deleted: your account, profile, speeches, mark, repetitions, and follows. Kept: nothing tied to your identity. Anonymized analytics events recorded before deletion can't be traced back to you.
